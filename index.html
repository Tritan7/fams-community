ABOUT US

EVOS Fams is a community-driven esports venture established for hosting esports league operations, gaming content production & distribution, and gaming talent management. Our vision is to create a better esports and gaming ecosystem for our region.

— WHAT WE DO

Esports Event & Venue Operation

Host esports events and game tournament. Manage and maintain the events and all.

Content Production & Distribution

Content creation remains one of the core aspects of esports.

Tournament Live Broadcasting

Live viewing experience is one important part of tournaments. We build dedicated studios for live streaming.

Localized Promotions

We emphasize equal importance to every associated brand. We are always on our toes to get your name out there in the competitive scene.

Gaming & Esports Platform

An integrated platform to host esports events and game tournaments. Manage and maintain the events seamlessly.
